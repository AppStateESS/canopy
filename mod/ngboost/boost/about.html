ngBoost (boost2)

By Matthew McNaney & Hilmar Runge

ngBoost is the module you are currently using. It allows the site admin to install, update, and uninstall various phpWebSite modules.

ngBoost should always be updated before any other module.

ngBoost (boost2) is an enhancement of module Boost with UIs in web2.0 stuff. In case ngBoost is not suitable with your web browser, please use Boost instead.
