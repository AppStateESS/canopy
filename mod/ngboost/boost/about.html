ngBoost (boost2)

By Hilmar Runge

ngBoost (boost2) is an enhancement of module Boost (classical) with UIs in web2.0 stuff. In case ngBoost is not suitable with your web browser, please use the classical Boost instead. ngBoost anycase requires classical Boost installed.

ngBoost also introduces the optional usage of Distro servers, to have selections from where to pickup new modules and updates.
