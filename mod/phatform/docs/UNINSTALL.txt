Uninstallation of phatform
--------------------------

MAKE SURE YOU BACKUP YOUR PHATFORM DATA BEFORE YOU CONTINUE!!

All phatform data is located in tables prefixed with mod_phatform_
and files located in the phatform/export and phatform/archive
directories.

1. Open your browser and point it to your phpwebsite installation.

2. Login as a deity user and select the 'Modules' link from the user
   menu.

3. Enter the Boost module and click the 'Uninstall' button next to
   the module titled 'Form Generator'.

4. Choose 'Yes' when prompted to confirm the uninstall.

5. You may now safely remove all the files in the ./mod/phatform/
   directory.
